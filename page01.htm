Fire Safety for Faculty and Staff in Patient Care Areas

Competency

All UMHS faculty and staff identify and prevent fire hazards and respond appropriately to fire incidents according to institutional safety requirements.

Critical Behaviors

All Clinicians must:

  1. Anticipate and prevent events that have the potential of causing patient/staff injury that include the combination of fuel, ignition, and oxidizers
  2. Know and use the following unit based fire safety resources
    • UMHHC Emergency Codes
    • RACE - Rescue, Alarm, Contain, and Extinguish
    • PASS - Pull, Aim, Squeeze and Sweep
    • Fire and Safety Tackboard
    • Fire Instruction Cards
    • Fire pull alarms
    • Fire extinguishers
    • Evacuation Plan, Smoke Compartments, and Refuge Sites
    • Evacuation door markers
    • Fire or Fire Drill Critique Form
    • Medical gas shutoff
  3. Rapidly respond to a fire or fire drill using RACE and PASS.
  4. Evacuate patients appropriate to the patient's health situation and location
