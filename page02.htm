UMHS Restricted Antimicrobial Policy

  • Limits the use of “high-risk” antibiotics (those that are most likely to lead to development of resistance and adverse outcomes, such as C. difficile infection) and states the criteria for their use
  • Use of "Tier 1" restricted antimicrobials requires prior approval by Infectious Disease (ID) or the Antimicrobial Stewardship Team (AST). Ongoing use is monitored by Pharmacy
  • Use of "Tier 2" restricted antimicrobials must comply with established restriction criteria. If criteria are not met, providers must call for AST approval or obtain ID consult
  • If use of any restricted antimicrobial continues without approval, AST is authorized to discontinue it
