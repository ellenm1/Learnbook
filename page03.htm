Microscope – Care and Maintenance

  1. The microscope should be covered when not in use to protect the objectives and oculars from dust.
  2. Clean the objective lens following each use with the lens cleaner and lens paper provided. 
    NOTE: Do not use a dry cloth, “Kleenex” or gauze when cleaning the oculars – this will scratch the sensitive glass surfaces.
  3. The 10X objective must be kept free from oil.
  4. An external agency is contracted to inspect, clean and align microscopes annually and upon request by calling Mager Scientific (734) 426-3885 .
  5. A label with the last inspection date is placed on the base of the microscope.
