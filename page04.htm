Reagents – Storage & Quality Control

10% Potassium Hydroxide (KOH)

  • Store at room temperature (15–30o C).
  • Examine solution for contamination semi-annually (at a minimum ):
    1. Add one drop of reagent on microscope slide and coverslip.
    2. Examine slide at 40X looking for yeast.
      • No yeast present in reagent – continue to use.
      • Yeast present in reagent – discard and obtain fresh reagent.

 

Saline Solution

  • Store at room temperature (15–30o C).
  • Examine solution for contamination semi-annually (at a minimum ):
    1. Add one drop of reagent on microscope slide and coverslip.
    2. Examine slide at 40X looking for yeast.
      • No yeast present in reagent – continue to use.
      • Yeast present in reagent – discard and obtain fresh reagent.
    3. Saline solution should be clear – cloudiness/turbidity may indicate bacterial contamination.  Discard any solution that appears cloudy.
